V가 m차원 Hilbert space고 W가 n차원 Hilbert space면 V⭙W는 mn차원 Hilbert space

V⭙W의 basis는 ∣i⟩⊗∣I⟩\ket{i}\otimes\ket{I}∣i⟩⊗∣I⟩, where ∣i⟩\ket{i}∣i⟩는 V의 basis, ∣I⟩\ket{I}∣I⟩는 W의 basis

A가 V의 operator, B가 W의 operator일 때 A⭙B는 V⭙W의 operator, A⊗B(∣v⟩⊗∣w⟩)=A∣v⟩⊗B∣w⟩A \otimes B (\ket{v}\otimes\ket{w}) = A\ket{v} \otimes B\ket{w}A⊗B(∣v⟩⊗∣w⟩)=A∣v⟩⊗B∣w⟩

Inner product는 (⟨v∣⊗⟨w∣)(∣v′⟩⊗∣w′⟩)=⟨v∣v′⟩⟨w∣w′⟩(\bra{v}\otimes\bra{w})(\ket{v'}\otimes\ket{w'}) = \braket{v \vert v'}\braket{w \vert w'}(⟨v∣⊗⟨w∣)(∣v′⟩⊗∣w′⟩)=⟨v∣v′⟩⟨w∣w′⟩

Tensor product notation

∣v⟩⊗∣w⟩=∣v⟩∣w⟩=∣v,w⟩=∣vw⟩\ket{v}\otimes\ket{w} = \ket{v}\ket{w} = \ket{v,w} = \ket{vw} ∣v⟩⊗∣w⟩=∣v⟩∣w⟩=∣v,w⟩=∣vw⟩

∣ψ⟩⊗2=∣ψ⟩⊗∣ψ⟩,∣ψ⟩⊗n=∣ψ⟩⊗⋯⊗∣ψ⟩\ket{\psi}^{\otimes 2} = \ket{\psi}\otimes\ket{\psi}, \ket{\psi}^{\otimes n} = \ket{\psi}\otimes\cdots\otimes\ket{\psi} ∣ψ⟩⊗2=∣ψ⟩⊗∣ψ⟩,∣ψ⟩⊗n=∣ψ⟩⊗⋯⊗∣ψ⟩
