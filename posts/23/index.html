Controlled Z gate

Recall) Z=[100−1]Z = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{bmatrix}Z=[10​0−1​]

Controlled Z gate는 둘 다 ∣1⟩\ket{1}∣1⟩일 때 하나를 −∣1⟩-\ket{1}−∣1⟩으로 만들어주는데, −∣1⟩⊗∣1⟩=∣1⟩⊗−∣1⟩-\ket{1}\otimes\ket{1} = \ket{1}\otimes-\ket{1}−∣1⟩⊗∣1⟩=∣1⟩⊗−∣1⟩이므로, Controlled Z gate는 symmetric하다.

No cloning theorem

∣ψ⟩⊗∣ϕ⟩→U∣ψ⟩⊗∣ψ⟩\ket{\psi} \otimes \ket{\phi} \xrightarrow{U} \ket{\psi} \otimes \ket{\psi}∣ψ⟩⊗∣ϕ⟩U​∣ψ⟩⊗∣ψ⟩인 U는 없음. (∣ϕ⟩\ket{\phi}∣ϕ⟩는 특정 초기 상태)

그런 U가 있다고 가정하면 U(∣0⟩⊗∣ϕ⟩)=∣0⟩⊗∣0⟩,U(∣1⟩⊗∣ϕ⟩)=∣1⟩⊗∣1⟩U(\ket{0} \otimes \ket{\phi}) = \ket{0}\otimes\ket{0}, U(\ket{1} \otimes \ket{\phi}) = \ket{1}\otimes\ket{1}U(∣0⟩⊗∣ϕ⟩)=∣0⟩⊗∣0⟩,U(∣1⟩⊗∣ϕ⟩)=∣1⟩⊗∣1⟩

∴U((α∣0⟩+β∣1⟩)⊗∣ϕ⟩)=α∣0⟩⊗∣0⟩+β∣1⟩⊗∣1⟩\therefore U((\alpha\ket{0}+\beta\ket{1}) \otimes \ket{\phi}) = \alpha\ket{0}\otimes\ket{0}+\beta\ket{1}\otimes\ket{1} ∴U((α∣0⟩+β∣1⟩)⊗∣ϕ⟩)=α∣0⟩⊗∣0⟩+β∣1⟩⊗∣1⟩

그런데 U((α∣0⟩+β∣1⟩)⊗∣ϕ⟩)=(α∣0⟩+β∣1⟩)⊗(α∣0⟩+β∣1⟩)U((\alpha\ket{0}+\beta\ket{1}) \otimes \ket{\phi}) = (\alpha\ket{0}+\beta\ket{1})\otimes(\alpha\ket{0}+\beta\ket{1})U((α∣0⟩+β∣1⟩)⊗∣ϕ⟩)=(α∣0⟩+β∣1⟩)⊗(α∣0⟩+β∣1⟩)여야 하므로 모순
