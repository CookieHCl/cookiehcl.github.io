Quantum oracle

∣x⟩∣q⟩→O∣x⟩∣q⊕f(x)⟩\ket{x}\ket{q} \xrightarrow{O} \ket{x}\ket{q \oplus f(x)}∣x⟩∣q⟩O​∣x⟩∣q⊕f(x)⟩

f(x)는 classical 회로이므로 quantum 회로로 만들 수 있음

x -> f(x) 만드는 회로를 U라고 하면, U 이후 f(x)와 q의 CNOT 이후 U-1 (uncomputation)을 해주면 ∣x⟩∣q⟩→O∣x⟩∣q⊕f(x)⟩\ket{x}\ket{q} \xrightarrow{O} \ket{x}\ket{q \oplus f(x)}∣x⟩∣q⟩O​∣x⟩∣q⊕f(x)⟩

Grover operator

∣α⟩=1N−M∑∣x⟩\ket{\alpha} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{N-M}}\sum\ket{x}∣α⟩=N−M​1​∑∣x⟩: uniform superposition of all non-solution states

∣β⟩=1M∑∣x⟩\ket{\beta} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{M}}\sum\ket{x}∣β⟩=M​1​∑∣x⟩: uniform superposition of all solution states

∣ψ⟩=1N∑∣x⟩=N−MN∣α⟩+MN∣β⟩\ket{\psi} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{N}}\sum\ket{x} = \frac{\sqrt{N-M}}{\sqrt{N}}\ket{\alpha} + \frac{\sqrt{M}}{\sqrt{N}}\ket{\beta}∣ψ⟩=N​1​∑∣x⟩=N​N−M​​∣α⟩+N​M​​∣β⟩: uniform superposition of all states
