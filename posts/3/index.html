Vector space

양자역학에선 복소수를 사용함

vector addition ∣V⟩+∣W⟩\ket{V} + \ket{W}∣V⟩+∣W⟩와 multiplication by scalar a∣V⟩a\ket{V}a∣V⟩ 연산이 있고, 아래 조건들을 만족하는 공간을 vector space라고 함

  • Closure: vector addition과 multiplication by scalar의 결과도 vector space에 속해야 함
  • Scala multiplication is distributive: a(∣V⟩+∣W⟩)=a∣V⟩+a∣W⟩,(a+b)∣V⟩=a∣V⟩+b∣V⟩a(\ket{V}+\ket{W}) = a\ket{V} + a\ket{W}, (a+b)\ket{V} = a\ket{V} + b\ket{V}a(∣V⟩+∣W⟩)=a∣V⟩+a∣W⟩,(a+b)∣V⟩=a∣V⟩+b∣V⟩
  • Scala multiplication is associative: a(b∣V⟩)=(ab)∣V⟩a(b\ket{V}) = (ab)\ket{V}a(b∣V⟩)=(ab)∣V⟩
  • Addition is commutative: ∣V⟩+∣W⟩=∣W⟩+∣V⟩\ket{V} + \ket{W} = \ket{W} + \ket{V}∣V⟩+∣W⟩=∣W⟩+∣V⟩
  • Addition is associative: ∣V⟩+(∣W⟩+∣Z⟩)=(∣V⟩+∣W⟩)+∣Z⟩\ket{V} + (\ket{W} + \ket{Z}) = (\ket{V} + \ket{W}) + \ket{Z}∣V⟩+(∣W⟩+∣Z⟩)=(∣V⟩+∣W⟩)+∣Z⟩
  • Existence of additive identity: ∣0⟩∈V\ket{0} \in V∣0⟩∈V such that ∣V⟩+∣0⟩=∣V⟩\ket{V} + \ket{0} = \ket{V}∣V⟩+∣0⟩=∣V⟩
  • Existence of additive inverse: ∣−V⟩∈V\ket{-V} \in V∣−V⟩∈V such that ∣V⟩+∣−V⟩=∣0⟩\ket{V} + \ket{-V} = \ket{0}∣V⟩+∣−V⟩=∣0⟩
  • Existence of scalar multiplication identity: 1∣V⟩=∣V⟩1\ket{V} = \ket{V}1∣V⟩=∣V⟩

Inner product

  • Inner product 결과는 복소수
  • Skew symmetry: ⟨V∣W⟩=⟨W∣V⟩∗\braket{V \vert W} = \braket{W \vert V}^*⟨V∣W⟩=⟨W∣V⟩∗
  • Positive semidefinite: ⟨V∣V⟩≥0\braket{V \vert V} \geq 0⟨V∣V⟩≥0 and ⟨V∣V⟩=0\braket{V \vert V} = 0⟨V∣V⟩=0 iff ∣V⟩=∣0⟩\ket{V} = \ket{0}∣V⟩=∣0⟩
  • Linearity in ket: ⟨V∣aW+bZ⟩=a⟨V∣W⟩+b⟨V∣Z⟩\braket{V \vert aW + bZ} = a\braket{V \vert W} + b\braket{V \vert Z}⟨V∣aW+bZ⟩=a⟨V∣W⟩+b⟨V∣Z⟩

위 조건들로부터 Antilinearity in bra ⟨aW+bZ∣V⟩=a∗⟨W∣V⟩+b∗⟨Z∣V⟩\braket{aW + bZ \vert V} = a^*\braket{W \vert V} + b^*\braket{Z \vert V}⟨aW+bZ∣V⟩=a∗⟨W∣V⟩+b∗⟨Z∣V⟩를 유도할 수 있음

Schwarz inequality

∣⟨V∣W⟩∣≤∣V∣∣W∣\lvert \braket{V \vert W} \rvert \leq \lvert V \rvert \lvert W \rvert ∣⟨V∣W⟩∣≤∣V∣∣W∣

∣∣V∣2W−⟨V∣W⟩V∣2=∣V∣2(∣V∣2∣W∣2−∣⟨V∣W⟩∣2)≥0\lvert \lvert V \rvert^2 W - \braket{V \vert W}V \rvert^2 = \lvert V \rvert^2 \left( \lvert V \rvert^2 \lvert W \rvert^2 - \lvert \braket{V \vert W} \rvert^2 \right) \geq 0 ∣∣V∣2W−⟨V∣W⟩V∣2=∣V∣2(∣V∣2∣W∣2−∣⟨V∣W⟩∣2)≥0

If ∣V∣≠0\lvert V \rvert \neq 0∣V∣=0, then ∣V∣2∣W∣2−∣⟨V∣W⟩∣2≥0\lvert V \rvert^2 \lvert W \rvert^2 - \lvert \braket{V \vert W} \rvert^2 \geq 0∣V∣2∣W∣2−∣⟨V∣W⟩∣2≥0

If ∣V∣=0\lvert V \rvert = 0∣V∣=0, ∣⟨V∣W⟩∣=∣V∣∣W∣=0\lvert \braket{V \vert W} \rvert = \lvert V \rvert \lvert W \rvert = 0∣⟨V∣W⟩∣=∣V∣∣W∣=0

∣V+W∣2=⟨V+W∣V+W⟩=∣V∣2+⟨V∣W⟩+⟨W∣V⟩+∣W∣2≤∣V∣2+2∣⟨V∣W⟩∣+∣W∣2≤∣V∣2+2∣V∣∣W∣+∣W∣2=(∣V∣+∣W∣)2\begin{align*} \lvert V + W \rvert^2 =& \braket{V+W \vert V+W}\\ =& \lvert V \rvert^2 + \braket{V \vert W} + \braket{W \vert V} + \lvert W \rvert^2 \\ \leq& \lvert V \rvert^2 + 2\lvert \braket{V \vert W} \rvert + \lvert W \rvert^2 \\ \leq& \lvert V \rvert^2 + 2\lvert V \rvert \lvert W \rvert + \lvert W \rvert^2 \\ =& \left( \lvert V \rvert + \lvert W \rvert \right)^2 \end{align*}∣V+W∣2==≤≤=​⟨V+W∣V+W⟩∣V∣2+⟨V∣W⟩+⟨W∣V⟩+∣W∣2∣V∣2+2∣⟨V∣W⟩∣+∣W∣2∣V∣2+2∣V∣∣W∣+∣W∣2(∣V∣+∣W∣)2​
